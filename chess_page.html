Chess


“When you find a good move, find a better one”


Chess is a war over the board. The objective is not to crush your opponent's game, but your opponent's mind. Although I'm only a beginner at chess, I find it interesting because it is all about how you analyse the game with a tactical mindset. When you play chess, you can only focus on the board you're playing on, and it helps in building focus.
